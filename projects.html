My Projects

Heatah Fajita Team Archive

Made as a tribute to a cancelled and now partially lost competitive Pokémon series on YouTube, this website hosts a database preserving around five hundred teams which spawned from it. Built through a combination of MySQL, JavaScript, and Python, it is one of the personal projects I am most passionate about.

Fire Emblem Shin Monshou No Nazo: Weapon Stats Randomizer

Another personal project of mine based around one of my favorite games, I made a program to randomize the stats of every obtainable weapon in the game. Built using Python, the ndspy library, and even a bit of assembly reading to determine where everything is, this is a work-in-progress that I hope to maybe release one day.
